Golds Gym

Gold's Gym is a worldwide chain of fitness centers, commonly known for its strength training and bodybuilding facilities. Gold's Gym is also known for its strong community of fitness enthusiasts, and having been home to famous bodybuilders, and fitness icons. Promoting a supportive environment where members can connect and motivate each other.

Facilities & Services

Most locations feature a wide range of weightlifting and cardio equipment, accommodating to all fitness levels. They offer many different classes, from high-intensity interval training, to yoga and dance-based workouts. Members can work with certified personal trainers to develop customized workout plans to their individual goals. Some locations also offer nutrition counseling and wellness programs to support members in achieving a balanced lifestyle.

Random Facts

  • Former bodybuilder and US Marine, Joe Gold opened up a gym in Venice California, in 1965.
  • Gold's Gym now has nealry 700 gyms in 29 countries.
  • Was one of the first gyms to franchise, and now offers franchising opportunities worldwide.
  • Many Gold's Gym locations host community events, fitness challenges, and competitions to promote health and wellness.
